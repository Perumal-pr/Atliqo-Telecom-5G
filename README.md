Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze the business impact of 5G implementation on Atliqo Telecom Services. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of revenue trends, subscriber growth, and plan-level performance before and after the 5G rollout.

🔹 Key Insights Delivered:

Total Revenue Analysis
Visualized overall revenue performance with a clear comparison of revenue before and after 5G launch to measure business growth and adoption impact.

Active Users Analysis
Tracked total active users and compared subscriber base growth pre- and post-5G implementation to evaluate customer acquisition and retention.

Revenue by Plans
Analyzed revenue contribution by different tariff plans to identify high-performing plans and customer preferences after 5G rollout.

Revenue Trend Over Time
Created time-series visualizations to monitor monthly/quarterly revenue trends and detect growth patterns, seasonality, and post-launch momentum.

Pre vs Post 5G Impact Analysis
Built comparative KPI cards and trend visuals to assess:

Revenue growth %

User growth %

ARPU changes

Shift in plan preferences

🔹 Business Value:

Helped stakeholders evaluate ROI of 5G rollout

Identified revenue-driving plans post-5G

Highlighted cities/segments showing maximum 5G adoption

Supported strategic pricing and expansion decisions

🔹 Tools Used:

Power BI (DAX, Data Modeling, KPI cards, Time Intelligence)

Data transformation using Power Query

Python Pandas for Data Validation

Sql for Metrics Validation 

Interactive slicers for period and plan-level filtering
